The Best Movies/web series according to Amritpreet Singh

My top picks

  • Chal Mera Putt

    A Punjabi comedy movie set in Canada, showcasing the struggles and hilarious moments of Punjabi immigrants living abroad.

  • 3 Idiots

    A Bollywood film that revolves around the lives of three engineering students, highlighting the challenges and pressures of the Indian education system.

  • Panchayat

    A popular web series that portrays the simple yet humorous life of villagers in India, focusing on rural politics and daily struggles in a small village.
